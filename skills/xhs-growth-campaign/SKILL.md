# Xiaohongshu Growth Campaign

Use this skill when a user asks for a Xiaohongshu or RedNote growth workflow,
including researching top posts, comparing cases, drafting content, or preparing
a publishing package.

## Workflow

1. Collect top notes for the topic and requested `top_n`.
2. Compare reusable patterns across hooks, structures, saves, and comments.
3. Generate a practical article draft tied to the follower-growth goal.
4. Prepare a draft or scheduled publishing package through the configured
   connector.

## Governance

Publishing-related runs should require human approval before execution. In this
demo the connector only creates a draft package and does not post to an external
account.
